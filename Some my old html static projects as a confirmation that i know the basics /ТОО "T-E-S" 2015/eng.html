OUR MAIN DIRECTION

"TRUCK ENGINE SERVICE" LLP specializes in repairments of diesel engines of internal combustion for technique of industrial and commercial purpose, sale of spare parts and engines assy. .

WE ARE ALONG TIME ON THIS MARKET

We offer a full range of services for engine overhauling, diagnostics, servicing of engines, to prevent long downtime of your equipment associated with engine malfunctions.

We are engaged in the supply and sale of spare parts for engines, as well as engines in collection at affordable prices, proper quality, guarantee and prompt delivery.

TECHNICAL SPECIALISTS

Technical specialists of our company have taken training in framework training programs of "cummins" company and have certain certificates. They are equipped with all the necessary equipment, tools, transport for implementation of tasks of any complexity and in any point of kazakhstan.

ENGINE WORKSHOP

In KARAGANDA,there is an equipped engine shop for performing engine overhauls in stationary conditions.

WAREHOUSE OF SPARE PARTS.

Always, in stock, there are spare parts for engine models "Cummins" KTA-19, KTTA-19, KTA-50, QSX-15, QSM-11, etc.
